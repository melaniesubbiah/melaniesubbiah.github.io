I'm Melanie Subbiah.

I am a 4th year Computer Science PhD student, advised in Natural Language Processing by Kathy McKeown at Columbia University. I work on narrative summarization and various aspects of text safety online. Prior to grad school, I worked on GPT-3 at OpenAI. Alongside my NLP research, I am interested in AI for climate work and opportunities to speak about and teach NLP.
